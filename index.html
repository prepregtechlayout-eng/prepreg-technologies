Welcome to Prepreg Technologies

At Prepreg Technologies Pvt. Ltd., we bring innovation in chip design, embedded systems, and product development, delivering high-quality solutions for industries worldwide. Our expertise includes schematic design capture using OrCAD and Altium, schematic review and verification, procurement and assembly BoM services, PCB design and Gerber generation with Allegro and Altium (supporting up to 28-layer boards), signal integrity and power integrity analysis, Ansys simulation, FAB and CAM support, PCBA support, board bring-up and testing, embedded software development, thermal simulation, web development, RF, digital and analog circuit design, and precise PCB library (footprint) creation. With our end-to-end capabilities, we ensure every project meets the highest quality standards from concept to production.

Services

At Prepreg Technologies, we offer comprehensive electronics design services covering every stage from concept to production. Our expertise includes schematic capture, high-speed PCB design, mixed-signal board design, FPGA board design, and high-current PCB design. We provide advanced pre-design support such as Signal Integrity (SI) and Power Integrity (PI) analysis, along with Ansys simulations to ensure optimal performance. Our post-design services include fabrication and CAM support, bare board validation, and complete assembly assistance. With a focus on precision, quality, and timely delivery, we ensure every project meets the highest industry standards.
